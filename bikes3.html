Rondo Hurt CF2 (2020)

With its HVRT, the Polish bike manufacturer is expanding the range to include an aerodynamically optimised racing bike. As with the Gravel models, Rondo uses the specially developed fork with the revolutionary TwinTip technology. This allows the wheel to be fitted at the front, in the two positions LO and HI. In the lower position the HVRT should be a comfortable training bike due to the more comfortable seating position. In addition, 650b tyres with a width of up to 47 mm can be driven in this setting. If you choose 700C tyres in the LO position, there is enough room to fit mudguards.In the HI setting, the HVRT should display its race genes and be more aggressive and agile with the more race-heavy seating position. A dream of a bike!
